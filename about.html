About Us

Something new

NOREXIT TEA BAR & DESSERTS

NOREXIT TEA BAR & DESSERTS is the perfect stop for those who love delicious bubble tea and a cozy atmosphere.
We take pride in serving freshly brewed tea, combined with a variety of toppings such as boba pearls, pudding, and fruit jelly, ensuring the best experience for our customers. With a modern style, a comfortable space, and a friendly staff, NOREXIT TEA BAR & DESSERTS promises to be the ideal place to meet friends, relax, and enjoy sweet moments.
Visit us today and discover the taste of happiness!
